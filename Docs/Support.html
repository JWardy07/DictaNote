Quick Help

If recording/transcription is not working, check permissions:

Settings → DictaNote → enable Microphone and Speech Recognition.

FAQ

How do I delete a note?
Tap Delete on the note card.

Can I export notes?
Yes. Tap Export to share/save as a text file.

Can example notes be removed?
Yes. Example notes can be deleted like any other note.

Is this a subscription app?
No. DictaNote is a one-time purchase with everything included.

Privacy

DictaNote requests microphone and speech recognition access only to record and transcribe your notes.
