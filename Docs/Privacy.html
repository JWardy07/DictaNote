Privacy Policy for DictaNote

This Privacy Policy explains how DictaNote handles your information when you use the app.

Who We Are

Developer: Jamie Ward

Contact: privacy@yourdomain.com

Data We Access

  • Microphone: used to record your voice notes.
  • Speech Recognition: used to convert your speech into text.

These permissions are requested only when needed for app features.

How Your Data Is Used

  • To record audio you choose to capture.
  • To transcribe audio into editable notes.
  • To save notes locally so you can view, edit, delete, and export them.

Data Sharing

DictaNote does not sell your personal data.

DictaNote does not require an account and does not include advertising SDKs.

Exported notes are shared only when you explicitly choose to export/share them.
